About

I'm a computer science engineer who moves between hardware and product, systems and story. At Ohio State — one of 36 students selected for the Integrated Business and Engineering Honors Program — I've learned that the most interesting problems live at intersections.

My work spans writing device drivers for defense R&D, deploying full-stack platforms for startups, and building real-time dashboards for an autonomous vehicle. What connects it all is the same instinct: build carefully, think through the whole system, care about how it feels to use.

Outside of engineering I'm drawn to things that reward attention — Pre-Raphaelite paintings, a good bouldering problem, card making, the ritual of a well-made latte. I think that care for craft in small things shows up in the code.
